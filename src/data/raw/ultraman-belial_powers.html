Ultraman Belial's powers and abilities

    Ultra Abilities – As a member of the Ultra Race, Belial has the following abilities.

  • Longevity – Due to their unique partial energy biology, Ultras possess no defined lifespan. As they age, an Ultra’s power increases dramatically. Belial is 160,000 years old.

  • Ultra Armor - An Ultra's skin is composed of Spacium, a highly durable material that is resistant to most kinds of damage, including lasers, fire and nuclear explosions.

  • Light Absorption – Ultras absorb and metabolize sunlight to power their full forms and abilities. In the Earth’s atmosphere (due to either pollution or other reasons), an Ultra can run for about 3 minutes before needing to retire (due to Belial's unique constitution, it is unknown if he possesses this limit).

  • Size Alteration – Ultras can change their size at will from microscopic to up to 55 meters (in Belial’s case).

  • Ultra Willpower – Ultras all possess a degree of telekinetic ability, usually matching their lifting capabilities.

  • Telepathy – Belial can communicate with other Ultras over vast interstellar distances.

  • Flight – All Ultras can fly in atmosphere and in space using anti-gravity waves they naturally produce. Belial can fly at Mach 22 in atmosphere.

  • Blending In – Ultras possess two ways they can integrate into human society. They can either join their energy essence with a human host or create a human body for their own use. Belial prefers his Reionics abilities to this.

  • Ultra Sign – Ultras can create light-based messages that only other Ultras can perceive.

  • Teleportation – By using large amounts of their energy reserves, Ultras can teleport themselves almost anywhere they please over interstellar distances.

  • Travel Sphere – Ultras can create travel spheres they use for general interstellar transportation. They presumably convert themselves into data for this process, as said by Ultraman X.

Reionics Form - After merging himself with Alien Reiblood, Belial gained several new abilities including a spiritual form and the ability to possess others and resist possession.

Deathcium Beam - Belial's version of an Ultra's energy attack and can be fired either from his palm or from the standard + position for increased power.

Energy Absorption - Belial can absorbing monsters and external energy sources to strengthen himself and achieve powerful transformations.

Dark Energy Empowerment - Belial can enhance his controlled monsters and followers with his own dark energy and even fuse them together to create stronger monsters.

Kaiser Claws - Belial's claws are very potent melee weapon with many abilities. They can deflect basic energy attacks and fire energy blasts and lightning from their tips. Cuts from the claws can also infect an individual with the Belial virus, which corrupts the target and puts them under Belial's control.

Strengthening - Belial can empower his punches and kicks with energy to greatly increase their potency and causing shockwaves to erupt from each hit.

Aura - Belial's malicious aura has both psychological effects on his opponents and causes darkness and storms to fill the sky.

Superhero Class

The Superhero Database Classification number, or SHDB Class, is a number that represents the overall 'power' of a character. All traits of a character are used for calculating the Classification.

What it DOESN'T mean

This doesn't mean that a higher class would always beat a lower class character. But the bigger the difference in Class is, the more obvious it is who'll win in a fight.

How is this calculated
( INT^1.3 + (STR*0.5 )^2 + (SPE*0.5)^2 + DUR^1.6 + (POW + (SPS*SPL))^2 + COM^1.8 ) ^ TIER
Super Power Score and Level

Every Super Power has a score (SPS) that is used to calculate the Class. Each Super Power also has 3 levels (SPL). The level is set when connecting that Super Power to a character. The level determines the final score, of the Super Power, being used in the calculation.
