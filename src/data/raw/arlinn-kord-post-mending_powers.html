Arlinn Kord's powers and abilities

Planeswalker - Arlinn is a Post-Mending Planeswalker, allowing her to freely travel through the Blind Eternities to other planes without being disintegrated. Her Spark also grants her immunities to various corrupting effects and raises many of her base resistances and giving her a much greater mana pool than an average being.

Lycanthropy - Arlinn is an Innistradian werewolf and transforms into her wolf form at night. Unlike most, she retains her human intellect and personality while transformed. She also possesses all of the normal werewolf associated abilities like increased durability, strength and senses.

    Voice of the Pack - Arlinn possesses a connection to the wild and nature through her green and red mana.

  • Wolf Control - Arlinn is very adept at controlling and guiding wolves and wolf-like creatures with most not requiring her to use magic to fight for her.

  • Enhancement - Arlinn can increase her own physical attributes in addition to her allies, even going so far as to grant them new abilities.

  • Avacynian Training - Prior to her transformation, Arlinn learned holy magic from the Avacynian church.

  • Summoning - Arlinn can summon allies to her from other planes, although she prefers her wolves to any other allies.

Superhero Class

The Superhero Database Classification number, or SHDB Class, is a number that represents the overall 'power' of a character. All traits of a character are used for calculating the Classification.

What it DOESN'T mean

This doesn't mean that a higher class would always beat a lower class character. But the bigger the difference in Class is, the more obvious it is who'll win in a fight.

How is this calculated
( INT^1.3 + (STR*0.5 )^2 + (SPE*0.5)^2 + DUR^1.6 + (POW + (SPS*SPL))^2 + COM^1.8 ) ^ TIER
Super Power Score and Level

Every Super Power has a score (SPS) that is used to calculate the Class. Each Super Power also has 3 levels (SPL). The level is set when connecting that Super Power to a character. The level determines the final score, of the Super Power, being used in the calculation.
