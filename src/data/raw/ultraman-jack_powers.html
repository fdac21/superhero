Ultraman Jack's powers and abilities

    Ultra Abilities – As a member of the Ultra Race, Jack has the following abilities.

  • Longevity – Due to their unique partial energy biology, Ultras possess no defined lifespan. As they age, an Ultra’s power increases dramatically. Jack is 17,000 years old.

  • Ultra Armor - An Ultra's skin is composed of Spacium, a highly durable material that is resistant to most kinds of damage, including lasers, fire and nuclear explosions.

  • Light Absorption – Ultras absorb and metabolize sunlight to power their full forms and abilities. In the Earth’s atmosphere (due to either pollution or other reasons), an Ultra can run for about 3 minutes before needing to retire.

  • Size Alteration – Ultras can change their size at will from microscopic to up to 40 meters (in Jack’s case).

  • Ultra Willpower – Ultras all possess a degree of telekinetic ability, usually matching their lifting capabilities.

  • Telepathy – Jack can communicate with other Ultras over vast interstellar distances.

  • Flight – All Ultras can fly in atmosphere and in space using anti-gravity waves they naturally produce. Jack can fly at Mach 5 in atmosphere.

  • Blending In – Ultras possess two ways they can integrate into human society. They can either join their energy essence with a human host or create a human body for their own use.

  • Ultra Sign – Ultras can create light-based messages that only other Ultras can perceive.

  • Teleportation – By using large amounts of their energy reserves, Ultras can teleport themselves almost anywhere they please over interstellar distances.

  • Travel Sphere – Ultras can create travel spheres they use for general interstellar transportation. They presumably convert themselves into data for this process, as said by Ultraman X.

    Energy Abilities - Jack can harness the energy he gains from light sources for a number of purposes.

  • Spacium Beam - Jack's standard energy attack. It more than capable of defeating most standard kaiju.

  • Ultra Slash - Jack creates a razor-sharp energy disc he uses to dismember foes.

  • Cinerama Shot - One of Jack's most powerful energy attacks, it can kill most monsters in a single hit.

  • Ultra Shot - This plasma beam does an incredible amount of damage and is capable of vaporizing a kaiju down to the bone.

  • Ultra Eye Light - Jack fires lasers from his eyes.

  • Ultra Rocket Bullets - Jack fires a series of explosive light bullets from his fingers.

  • Ultra Flash - Ultraman Jack can unleash a series of explosive flashes from his hands.

  • Stop Beam/Flash - These beams stop a target in its tracks and paralyzes them.

  • Ultra Barrier - Jack can create a force field that can deflect attacks. He can also fire it as a beam to encase a target.

    Miscellaneous

  • Ultra Frost - Jack can spray a freezing mist from his hands.

  • Ultra Power - Jack can temporarily increase his strength to break a hold.

  • Ultra Spin - Jack can spin his body rapidly to turn himself into a drill or to create a vortex to suck enemies in.

  • Fluroscope Beam - Jack can emit a light from his eyes that disables invisibility and camouflage.

Ultraman Jack's weaknesses

Electricity - Jack has shown a weakness to electricity based attacks.

Superhero Class

The Superhero Database Classification number, or SHDB Class, is a number that represents the overall 'power' of a character. All traits of a character are used for calculating the Classification.

What it DOESN'T mean

This doesn't mean that a higher class would always beat a lower class character. But the bigger the difference in Class is, the more obvious it is who'll win in a fight.

How is this calculated
( INT^1.3 + (STR*0.5 )^2 + (SPE*0.5)^2 + DUR^1.6 + (POW + (SPS*SPL))^2 + COM^1.8 ) ^ TIER
Super Power Score and Level

Every Super Power has a score (SPS) that is used to calculate the Class. Each Super Power also has 3 levels (SPL). The level is set when connecting that Super Power to a character. The level determines the final score, of the Super Power, being used in the calculation.
