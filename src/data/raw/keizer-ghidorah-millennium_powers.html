Keizer Ghidorah's powers and abilities

Transformation - Keizer Ghidorah has three forms, each of which has a specific utility.

    Gorath Form - The Gorath form is primarily for interstellar travel.

  • Travel - While in this rocky form, Keizer Ghidorah is capable at traveling through space at extreme speeds.

  • Devastating Impact - The explosion from Gorath being destroyed pre-impact was enough to destroy Tokyo and much of the surrounding area. Had is truly impacted, assuming a 1 KM diameter, it would have been the equivalent of 926 Tsar Bomba explosions.

    Monster X Form - Keizer Ghidorah's more nimble form.

  • Physicality - Monster X is a very nimble and martially talented form, dodging Godzilla's attacks and using wrestling-like moves to brutally beat down Godzilla.

  • Exoskeleton - Monster X is coated in a durable exoskeleton that allows him to shrug off most hits from Godzilla, although it was cut through by Gigan's blades.

  • Destroyed Thunder - Monster X can fire lightning-like beams from his eyes and the eyes on his skull pauldrons. The beams appear to be roughly equivalent to Godzilla's atomic breath.

  • Levitation - Monster X can levitate to reposition himself in battle.

  • Mode Switch - Keizer Ghidorah can switch between this mode and his true form at will.

    Keizer Ghidorah Form - Keizer Ghidorah's "true" form.

  • Massive Size - Although Keizer Ghidorah lacks Monster X's exoskeleton and agility, he trades that for sheer bulk and power. He was capable of easily lifting the 60K ton Godzilla above his head and hold GZ with little effort.

  • Flight - Keizer Ghidorah is capable of true flight while in this form, although it is rarely shown.

  • Destroyed Keizer - Keizer Ghidorah's version of the Destroyed Thunder. It is far more powerful than Destroyed Thunder in addition to allowing him to use it to levitate objects.

  • Energy Drain - By sinking his fangs into an opponent or object, Keizer Ghidorah is capable of draining the target of its energy and adding it to his own.

  • Mode Switch - Keizer Ghidorah can switch from this form to the Monster X form at will.

Keizer Ghidorah's weaknesses

Decapitation - Keizer Ghidorah loses much of his power should he lose one of his heads.

Malice - Keizer Ghidorah's sheer malice can lead to him getting overconfident and this can be exploited.

Superhero Class

The Superhero Database Classification number, or SHDB Class, is a number that represents the overall 'power' of a character. All traits of a character are used for calculating the Classification.

What it DOESN'T mean

This doesn't mean that a higher class would always beat a lower class character. But the bigger the difference in Class is, the more obvious it is who'll win in a fight.

How is this calculated
( INT^1.3 + (STR*0.5 )^2 + (SPE*0.5)^2 + DUR^1.6 + (POW + (SPS*SPL))^2 + COM^1.8 ) ^ TIER
Super Power Score and Level

Every Super Power has a score (SPS) that is used to calculate the Class. Each Super Power also has 3 levels (SPL). The level is set when connecting that Super Power to a character. The level determines the final score, of the Super Power, being used in the calculation.
